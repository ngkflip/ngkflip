Why We Use Amazon Affiliate Links

At NGKFlip, we use Amazon affiliate links to earn a small commission on products purchased through our website. This commission is essential for maintaining our site and providing you with high-quality content and valuable recommendations.

NGKFlip participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, which is an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for websites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com

How It Works

When you click on an Amazon affiliate link on NGKFlip, it contains a unique tracking code. If you make a purchase on Amazon within a specified period after clicking the link, we receive a commission.
